Types of Devices

In this part you'll familiarize yourself with some common networking devices and what they do

Router

Routers are like traffic directors, sending data where it needs to go. They make sure your computer connects to the right website.

Switch

A switch is like a power strip, but for networks. It connects devices within a LAN so they can talk to each other.

Modem

Modems connect you to the internet. Think of it like a bridge that connects your home to the world.
