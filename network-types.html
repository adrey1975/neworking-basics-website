Types of Networks

Let's take a look some of the different type of networks that exist and that you should familiarize yourself with.

LAN (Local Area Network)

A network covering a small geographic area, like a home or office.

WAN (Wide Area Network)

A network that extends over large distances, connecting multiple LANs.

PAN (Personal Area Network)

This could be as small as a Bluetooth connection between your phone and your earbuds.

MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

Think of it like a network that could connect all the public libraries in a city.
